Inside the playbook

Eight clear steps. Nothing extra.

No fluff. No theory dump. Just the eight things that mattered when I built Edge Within from a back garden with zero followers.

01

Find Your Why

Get past "I want an audience" and lock the honest reason. When your why is real, showing up stops feeling optional.

02

Name & Identity

Pick a name and identity that still feels right six months from now. Not something you outgrow after episode four.

03

Format & Commitment

Choose a format you can keep. Commitment math beats ambition. Consistency compounds. Perfection doesn't.

04

Build Your Setup

Minimal gear, folder structure, Auphonic settings that work. Start recording this week. Not after you buy a studio.

05

Pre-Record Mindset

The ritual that gets you and your guest into the right state before the red light. State over scripts.

06

The Conversation

How to run conversations that create moments people remember. Moments they clip, share, and come back for.

07

Launch

Drop your first episodes without overthinking it. Stack 3 to 5 before you go public so launch day feels like an arrival, not a test.

08

Sustain & Grow

The weekly rhythm, guest pipeline, and habits that turn quiet early days into real momentum.
