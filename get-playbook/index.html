THE 8-STEP PATH

Structure that makes the transformation repeatable.

Not theory. The exact sequence I followed that let me grow the show while growing as a person.

01

Mindset First: Align with Your Identity & Why

Stop performing. Get clear on who you are and why this matters to you. This is the foundation that makes every conversation honest.

02

Design for Your Actual Capacity

Build something that can survive your real life. Ambition without capacity destroys the very growth you're chasing.

03

Build a Guest Relationship System

Create a pipeline where quality compounds. Give people a real reason to say yes that has nothing to do with your numbers.

04

Set Up Stupidly Simple Systems

Minimal gear and processes. Remove friction so you can keep showing up week after week when it matters most.

05

Master Pre-Record State & The Conversation

The ritual that gets you and your guest in the right headspace. How to lead conversations that create moments worth clipping and that help you grow.

06

Build One Repeatable Rhythm

Combine recording, editing, and reviewing into one sustainable weekly system. The rhythm itself becomes the teacher.

07

Launch

Stack episodes and get it out there so launch day feels like an arrival, not a test of your worth.

08

Grow & Earn in Alignment

Build real momentum and monetize in a way that matches your identity and protects the life you're building.

Same order I used. Do them in sequence and the outer growth supports the inner work instead of stealing from it.
