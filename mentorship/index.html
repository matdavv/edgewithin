Why this exists

I sat on the idea longer than I care to admit.

Starting is easy. Growing is where most people stall. Not because they lack talent. Because they don't have a sequence.

Day one for Edge Within was a back garden under trees. No studio. No audience. No paid ads. What I had was a clear order: what to do next, what to ignore, and how to keep showing up when the quiet early days tried to talk me out of it.

I learned the foundations from mentors including Chris Griffin, host of The Pocket Podcast. This program is that foundation plus what I ran on my own show: the tools, the clip craft, the guest pipeline, and the weekly rhythm.

No income guarantees. What you get is a system, a community, and optional direct access while you build a show you're proud of.
