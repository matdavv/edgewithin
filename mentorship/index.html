Common Traps

The mistakes that slow podcasts down.

Most new hosts fall into the same patterns. Recognise them early and you'll move faster.

  • Posting clips without a repeatable format
  • Spending hours editing before understanding what audiences actually watch
  • Treating Instagram and the podcast as two separate projects
  • Treating the show like a side project instead of something worth showing up for consistently

Fix the sequence (clips first, consistency second, guests third) and momentum builds.
