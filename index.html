Meet Mat

Why I started this.

In all honesty, I wanted to get better at listening. Better at communicating. I wanted deep conversations with people I admired.

I'm a curious guy. I wanted to know more, while expanding and meeting my own edge. That meant building something, consistently, from scratch.

I launched Edge Within on January 20, 2026. Zero followers, no audience, no plan beyond the next 7 days. But a clear system for how to think about it.

Every guest, every clip, every conversation has been built from scratch, in real time, with intention. No paid ads. Just consistency, curiosity, and showing up when the moment gets real.
