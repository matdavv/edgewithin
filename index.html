About the Show

Meet your edge within.

Edge Within is a long-form interview podcast built on one idea: the most important conversations are the ones most people avoid.

Every episode goes deep. One guest, one conversation, no agenda. Mindset, identity, resilience, growth, what it means to live on your own terms. The conversations that change how you think.

Hosted by Mat Davenport from Australia. Launched January 2026. Zero dollars in advertising. The audience grew because the conversations were worth sharing.
